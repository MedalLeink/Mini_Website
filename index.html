ABOUT US

At Leine Coffee, we’re passionate about crafting more than just coffee — we create warm moments with every cup. From ethically sourced beans to our cozy atmosphere, everything we do is inspired by a love for rich flavors and meaningful connections. Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso or settling in for a slow sip, LeiCo is your perfect daily escape.
